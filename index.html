Driving Nigeria's Clean Transport Future

The Professional Transport Officers Association of Nigeria (PTOAN) is a national association focused on transforming Nigeria's transport sector through the introduction, regulation, and promotion of Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) vehicles and Electric Vehicles (EVs).

Join PTOAN Today

About PTOAN

PTOAN is a newly established national association with a vision to revolutionize Nigeria's transportation landscape through sustainable energy solutions.

Our Mission

To lead Nigeria's transition to clean transport by promoting CNG and EV adoption, establishing safety standards, and providing professional training nationwide.

Our Vision

A Nigeria with a sustainable, efficient, and environmentally-friendly transport system that leverages the nation's natural gas resources and embraces electric mobility.

Our Objectives

Regulate CNG/EV standards, facilitate training programs, promote nationwide awareness, advocate for supportive policies, and foster international partnerships.

CNG & EV Advocacy

Promoting clean transport alternatives specifically tailored for Nigeria's unique challenges and opportunities.

Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) for Nigeria

Cost-Effective Fuel

CNG is significantly cheaper than petrol or diesel, reducing operating costs by up to 60% for transport operators.

Environmental Benefits

CNG produces 20-30% less CO2 emissions compared to petrol and reduces harmful particulate matter.

Utilizing Local Resources

Nigeria has abundant natural gas reserves, making CNG a strategic energy source for transportation.

Electric Vehicles (EVs) for Nigeria

Zero Emissions

EVs produce no tailpipe emissions, improving urban air quality and public health in Nigerian cities.

Lower Maintenance

EVs have fewer moving parts than combustion engines, reducing maintenance costs and downtime.

Renewable Integration

EV charging can be integrated with Nigeria's growing solar energy infrastructure for sustainable mobility.

Training & Safety Standards

Certification Programs

PTOAN offers certified training programs for CNG/EV mechanics, technicians, and safety inspectors.

Safety Protocols

Developing and implementing Nigeria-specific safety standards for CNG/EV infrastructure and operations.

Awareness Campaigns

Nationwide public awareness initiatives to educate Nigerians about clean transport benefits and safety.

Strategic Partnerships

PTOAN is actively engaged with international and local partners to advance Nigeria's clean transport agenda.

EV Manufacturers

Ongoing discussions with leading global EV companies to establish local assembly plants and service centers in Nigeria.

CNG Infrastructure

Collaborating with international energy firms to develop Nigeria's CNG refueling network and conversion centers.

Government Agencies

Working with federal and state transport ministries to develop supportive policies and regulations for clean transport.

Training Institutions

Partnering with technical schools and universities to develop curriculum for CNG/EV maintenance and safety.
